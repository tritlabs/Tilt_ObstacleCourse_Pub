Privacy Policy

Tilt Obstacle Course · Trit Labs, LLC
Effective date: July 1, 2026 · Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trit Labs, LLC ("we," "us") handles information in connection with the mobile game Tilt Obstacle Course (the "App"). We designed the App to collect as little personal information as possible. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask you for your name, email address, or other directly identifying information.

Age requirement

The App carries a 13+ ("Teen") age rating and is intended for teenagers and adults. By downloading or using the App, you confirm that you are at least 13 years old. If you are under 13, please do not use the App or provide any information through it. The App is not offered in Apple's Kids Category.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information that you provide directly. The App and the third-party services it uses collect the following automatically:

  • Gameplay analytics (anonymous). We use Mixpanel to understand how the game is played (for example: boards started and cleared, times, and in-app events). This is tied only to an anonymous, randomly generated identifier — not to your name or identity. You can turn this off at any time in Settings → Privacy → Share anonymous analytics.
  • Advertising data. The App shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may access device identifiers — including the Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) only if you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt — along with ad-interaction and general device/usage data. If you do not grant permission, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. Ads are configured to serve content rated suitable for a teen (13+) audience.
  • Apple Game Center (optional). If you sign in to Game Center, Apple manages your player identity to provide leaderboards and achievements. That data is handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy; we receive only the scores and achievement progress needed for those features.
  • Purchases. In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details; we only learn whether a purchase was completed.
  • On-device data. Your game progress, records, and settings are stored locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the game and its difficulty balance.
  • To display and measure advertising that supports the free version of the App.
  • To provide optional Game Center leaderboards, achievements, and in-app purchases.

Advertising and tracking choices

Before showing personalized ads, the App requests your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, and — where required by law (for example, in the European Economic Area and the UK) — presents a consent form through Google's User Messaging Platform. You can change your choices at any time:

  • Tracking: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Analytics: in-app Settings → Privacy.
  • Ad personalization: managed through the consent options presented in-app where applicable.

Where an ad-free version of the App is offered and purchased, ads and ad-related data collection are disabled.

Third-party services

The App relies on the following services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob & Google User Messaging Platform — policies.google.com/privacy (see also how Google uses data from apps that use its services).
  • Mixpanel — mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Apple (Game Center, App Store) — apple.com/legal/privacy.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to object to certain processing (including under the EU/UK GDPR and the California Consumer Privacy Act). Because we do not collect information that identifies you by name, we may be unable to associate data with a specific individual; however, you can exercise the controls described above, and you may contact us with any request.

Data retention

On-device data remains until you delete the App or clear its data. Data held by our third-party providers is retained according to their respective policies.

Children and teens

Consistent with its 13+ ("Teen") age rating, the App is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed to, or intended for, children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and the App is not offered in Apple's Kids Category. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it; a parent or guardian who believes their under-13 child has used the App may contact us at the address below.

Teens and the age of consent. In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and some other regions, the minimum age at which a person can consent to data processing ranges from 13 to 16. If you are below that age where you live, applicable law may require a parent's or guardian's involvement, and the App presents its consent options accordingly through Google's User Messaging Platform.

Advertising to minors. Ads are configured so that ad content is suitable for the App's 13+ rating. Personalized advertising depends on the permissions and consent you provide (see "Advertising and tracking choices" above); where applicable law restricts personalized or targeted advertising to minors, ads are provided on a non-personalized basis.

International transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than your own. Those transfers are handled under the providers' own safeguards as described in their privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, within the App.
